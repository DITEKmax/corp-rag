## Score-Only Robustness

- Added limited RAGAS retry controls: `ragas_max_retries=1`, `ragas_max_wait=5`.
- Replaced all-at-once scoring with per-record/per-metric scoring for the score-only path.

- The accepted post-fix run had `ragas_score_failures=[]`; all four RAGAS metrics scored `18/18` answered rows.
